Afghan Kofta

Afghan Kofta are deliciously spiced meatballs, typically made from ground lamb or beef mixed with onions, garlic, and spices. These are often served with naan or over rice with a side of chutney.

Baklava

Baklava is a famous Turkish dessert made from layers of thin filo pastry filled with chopped nuts, sweetened with syrup or honey. It’s crispy, sweet, and indulgent, often served with tea or coffee.

Shawarma

Shawarma is one of Dubai’s most famous street foods. It consists of thinly sliced meat (chicken or beef) wrapped in flatbread with garlic sauce, pickles, and salad. It’s quick, flavorful, and perfect for on-the-go eating.

Karahi

Karahi is a popular Pakistani dish, cooked in a wok-like pan called "karahi." It's made with chicken or mutton, tomatoes, green chilies, and a variety of spices. The dish has a thick, flavorful gravy and is served with naan or roti.

Haleem

Haleem is a thick, nutritious stew made from a blend of wheat, barley, lentils, and meat, cooked slowly to create a smooth and flavorful dish. It's often garnished with fried onions, ginger, and lemon, adding layers of texture and taste.
